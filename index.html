About

Named for the shepherd moon

Daphnis is one of Saturn's smallest moons — just 8 kilometers across. Yet as it orbits within the Keeler Gap, its gravity sculpts visible waves across Saturn's rings, disturbances thousands of times its own size.

We founded Daphnis LLC on that same principle: that focused, purposeful work can create outsized impact. We're a small team building tools that reshape how humans and artificial intelligence interact socially.
